🌐 International Festival of Youth 2026

Taking YourFin to the world.

September 11–17 Ekaterinburg, Russia

In 2024, the World Youth Festival brought together young people from around the world to strengthen international ties. The event inspired the creation of International Friendship Clubs (IFC) — unique associations of young, active people who implement joint initiatives across borders.

IFC participants exchange experiences and carry out cross-cultural projects. At IFY 2026 in Ekaterinburg, YourFin will be presented as an open educational framework — to gather insights from youth in 180+ countries on how financial illiteracy affects their lives.

The gap between financial stability and struggle is almost never about intelligence. It is almost always about access. YourFin is our answer to that gap.
